AirWriter Project

B.Tech CSE Capstone Project - Hand Gesture Writing System

Write or paint in mid-air using your webcam. Please follow the gesture constraints:

  • Point Write: Draw by pointing your index finger (fold other fingers). Flat palm hovers.
  • Pinch Write: Draw by pinching your index and thumb (holding virtual pen). Release to hover.
  • Air Buttons: Hover your cursor over the CLEAR, ERASER, UNDO, SAVE buttons at the top of the canvas for 1.2 seconds to trigger them.
